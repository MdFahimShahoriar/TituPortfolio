Hi! I'm Md. Fahim Shahoriar Titu, a researcher exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity. I graduated in CSE from North South University (2024) and currently work as a Researcher at ZAR Consultancy and as a Cybersecurity IT Audit Associate at ACNABIN.

I have published multiple papers under the supervision of Dr. Riasat Khan. My work focuses on building explainable and secure intelligent systems.

Research Interests: (I) Explainable AI (XAI) & Vision Transformers, (II) Post-Quantum Cryptography (Kyber), (III) Edge AI for Real-Time Detection, (IV) Secure Image Communication.

Open to collaborations in AI-driven security and computer vision.
