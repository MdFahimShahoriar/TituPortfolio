Research Interests

Bridging AI, Security, and Computer Vision

Explainable AI (XAI) & Vision Transformers

Developing transparent intelligent systems using Vision Transformers (ViT) and Explainable AI techniques. My work leverages Grad-CAM and DINOv2 to make "black-box" deep learning models interpretable for sensitive applications like fine-grained vehicle damage assessment.

XAI Vision Transformers Grad-CAM DINOv2

Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)

Designing hybrid encryption frameworks that combine Post-Quantum Cryptography (Kyber) with chaotic systems. I aim to secure digital image communication against future quantum computing threats, ensuring robust data integrity and confidentiality in transmission.

Kyber Post-Quantum Chaos Theory Encryption

Edge AI & Real-Time Detection

Optimizing lightweight deep learning models for resource-constrained edge devices, specifically drones. My research enables real-time inferencing for critical tasks like fire detection and visual pollution monitoring with minimal latency and high efficiency.

Edge Computing Drones YOLO Real-Time

Medical Image Analysis

Applying advanced computer vision to healthcare. I develop fusion models using knowledge distillation and image filtering to improve the accuracy of medical diagnoses, such as root canal failure and kidney tumor detection, bridging the gap between AI and clinical practice.

Medical Imaging Knowledge Distillation CNN Healthcare
